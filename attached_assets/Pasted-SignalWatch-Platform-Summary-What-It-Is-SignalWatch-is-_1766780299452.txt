SignalWatch Platform Summary
What It Is
SignalWatch is a B2B business intelligence platform designed for editorial teams monitoring the poultry, feed, and pet food industries. It tracks 900+ global companies, collecting and analyzing business signals (news, funding, executive changes, product launches, partnerships) using AI-powered analysis.

Data Storage
PostgreSQL Database - All company profiles, signals, alerts, and team activity stored persistently
Company Records: ~900 companies across 3 industry verticals (Poultry, Feed, Pet Food)
Signals: Business events with AI-generated analysis, sentiment scores, priority ratings, and source attribution
Core Capabilities
Feature	Description
Company Monitoring	Track companies individually, by industry group, or all at once with real-time progress indicators
AI Signal Analysis	Perplexity AI extracts entities, sentiment, priority, and actionable insights from news
Three-Level Triggers	Dashboard-wide, industry group, or individual company monitoring
Article Generation	Create news articles, briefs, or analysis pieces from signals using AI
CMS Export	Export to WordPress, Contentful, Markdown, or JSON with clickable source links
RSS Feeds	/api/rss/poultry, /api/rss/feed, /api/rss/pet-food, /api/rss/all
Collaborative Workflow	Team features for 3-person editorial team with activity tracking
Alert System	Configure notifications for specific signal types
Expansion Recommendations
Scheduled Monitoring - Add automated daily/weekly monitoring via cron jobs
Email Digests - Send daily signal summaries to team members
Custom RSS Filters - Allow filtering by signal type, priority, or sentiment
Competitive Analysis - Compare signals across competing companies
Dashboard Analytics - Charts showing signal trends, industry activity over time
Saved Searches - Let users save and rerun specific monitoring queries
Webhook Integrations - Push signals to Slack, Teams, or custom endpoints
Multi-language Support - Monitor international sources in multiple languages
